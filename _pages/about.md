# About me
I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the [Temple University Department of Physics](https://phys.cst.temple.edu), 
supervised by Prof. [Qimin Yan](https://phys.cst.temple.edu/qimin-yan.html) \[ [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ysfTfdkAAAAJ&hl=en) \]. 
My research interests include:
  * graph neural network representation of crystalline materials and prediction of their properties; 
  * first princles calculations of materials by applying *Density Funtional Theory*;
  * relation and correlation between physics and machine learning;

Before Ph.D. study, I received Bachelor of Science in Physics and Master of Philosophy in Physics from the [Chinese University of Hong Kong](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk). 

I'm interested in all intellectually challenging problems originated from physics and related to mathematics and computer science.
